Main Street Muncher

Drive an Ikarus 260. Munch the green dots. Dodge the cones.

About

Main Street Muncher turns real OpenStreetMap streets into a bite-sized maze. Zoom into a neighborhood, press GO, and ride the routes — collecting lime dots while construction cones try to catch you.

Street ends wrap to the far side so every road stays clearable. Power dots let you flatten cones for good. Arrow keys or WASD to steer.

Map data © OpenStreetMap contributors. Not affiliated with BKK or Ikarus.
