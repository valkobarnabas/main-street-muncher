About

Main Street turns real OpenStreetMap streets into a bite-sized maze. Zoom into a neighborhood, press GO, and ride the routes — collecting dots while construction cones try to catch you.

Street ends wrap to the far side so every road stays clearable. Power dots let you flatten cones for good. Arrow keys or WASD to steer. Use Customize to swap icons and colors.

Map data © OpenStreetMap contributors.
