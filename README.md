# Street Dots

Turn any OpenStreetMap neighborhood into a playable street maze. Zoom to a dense grid, press **Play**, collect the dots, and dodge the chasers. Based off the 2015/17 Google Maps April Fools' Pac-Man pranks.

## How to play

1. Search or pan/zoom to a city street grid.
2. Press the yellow **Play** button (bottom right).
3. Arrow keys or WASD to move.
4. Eat every yellow dot; power dots briefly let you chase the chasers.
5. **Back to map** exits play mode.
